Slow Cooker Blueberry French Toast

Description

Weekend brunch was never about "the healthy choice"... embrace it! This slow cooker Blueberry French Toast fits perfectly in your relaxed weekend plans. Treat yourself!

This recipe is from the folks at Recipes that Crock! Many thanks to them!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup brown sugar packed
  • 1 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup butter melted
  • 12 slices white bread
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen blueberries
  • 5 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups whole milk
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Garnish: whipped cream additional blueberries, powdered sugar

Steps

  1. Mix together your brown sugar, butter and cinnamon
  2. Spray your crock pot with cooking spray
  3. Sprinkle 1/3 of your brown sugar mixture evenly across the bottom of your crock pot
  4. Place 6 of your bread slices on top
  5. Sprinkle another 1/3 of your brown sugar mixture on top
  6. Put your blueberries evenly across the top
  7. Place your remaining bread on top
  8. Sprinkle the rest of your brown sugar mixture on top
  9. In a separate bowl, mix together your eggs,milk, vanilla and salt
  10. Pour evenly over everything
  11. Use a spoon to gently press your bread down
  12. Put your cover on your crock pot and refrigerate overnight
  13. The next morning, put your crock into your slow cooker, cover and cook on low for 3-4 hours (it is done when it is set and golden on top)
  14. Serve sprinkled with powdered sugar and top with a dollop of whipped cream and some blueberries
